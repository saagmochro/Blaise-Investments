About Us

Who we are

Blaise Investments Limited is a Kenyan company committed to providing high-quality products and professional services that meet the needs of individuals, businesses, and organizations. Established with a vision of reliability and integrity, we specialize in supply and distribution of corporate uniforms, protective wear, footwear, promotional items, camping gear, and related merchandise. With a focus on quality assurance, timely delivery, and competitive pricing, we have positioned ourselves as a trusted partner for clients across different sectors.

Our Story

We combine experience, innovation and customer focus to create value. Our team is dedicated to excellence and long-term partnerships.

Why Choose Us

  • Wide product range including uniforms, footwear, protective wear, knitwear, and promotional items.
  • Commitment to quality, durability, and customer satisfaction.
  • Reliable supply chain ensuring timely delivery for bulk and custom orders.
  • Competitive prices without compromising standards.
  • Integrity, accountability, and a client-first approach in every transaction.
